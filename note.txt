
**Comparison Games:**
- **Bigger or Smaller?** - Show two countries, guess which has larger population/area
- **Older or Newer?** - If you have founding dates, compare country ages
- **Richer or Poorer?** - Compare GDP/economy statistics

**Matching Games:**
- **Country to Capital** - Name the capital city
- **Country to Language** - Match nations to their primary languages
- **Country to Government Type** - Match countries to their government systems
- **Country to Region** - Identify which continent/region a country belongs to

**Trivia/Facts:**
- **Multiple Choice Facts** - "What is the capital of [Country]?" with 4 options
- **True/False Statements** - "Does [Country] border [Other Country]?"
- **Statistics Trivia** - "What is the population of [Country]?" with option ranges

**Map Game** (if you have a map):
- **Click the Country** - Show a name, click the correct region on the map

**What specific statistics do you have available?** Things like:
- Capitals/major cities
- Populations
- Area/size
- Languages
- Government types
- Borders/neighbors
- Currency
- Founding dates
- Regional groupings
